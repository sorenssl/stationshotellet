Our Story

A Railway Station Reborn

Stations Hotellet is a lovingly restored railway station transformed into a comfortable and charming apartment. Located on Västra Järnvägsgatan in Öjebyn, the building carries a rich history while offering all modern comforts.

Hosted by Doushka & Sören Stenvall, we take pride in providing a warm, welcoming environment — whether you're here for work or leisure.

Local guide

Things to do near Stations Hotellet

Northern Sweden has more to offer than you might think — here's what's nearby.

🏖️

Pite Havsbad

Sweden's largest beach resort, ~5 km from us. Water park, white sand beach, summer activities for all ages.

pitehavsbad.se →
🌌

Aurora Borealis

September to March. Öjebyn's clear, dark skies make for excellent northern lights viewing — sometimes right from the doorstep.

swedishlapland.com →
☀️

Midnight Sun

June to July. 24-hour daylight — perfect for evening hikes, late-night fishing, and sunsets that never quite end.

🏘️

Piteå Old Town

5 minutes by car. Charming wooden architecture, riverside cafés, the historic Piteå church.

visitpitea.se →
⚡

Markbygden Wind Park

30 km north — one of Europe's largest onshore wind parks. Tours available for energy enthusiasts and photographers.

🐕

Dog Sledding & Snowmobile

December to April. Husky tours, snowmobile safaris, and ice fishing on local lakes — the full Lapland experience.

swedishlapland.com →
⛷️

Lindbäcksstadion

Main local ski area, 11 km. Three alpine runs, 2 lifts, FunPark, illuminated cross-country trails, and a biathlon range.

pitea.se →
🌊

Storforsen rapids

~80 km north — one of Europe's largest unregulated rapids. 5 km long, 82 m drop. Trails, viewing ramps, summer café. 150,000 visitors/year.

visitalvsbyn.se →
